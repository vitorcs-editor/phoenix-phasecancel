Mental clarity is something many people take for granted until they notice it starting to fade. Moments of forgetfulness, difficulty concentrating, or feeling mentally foggy are signs that your brain may need more support. The good news is that the brain has an incredible ability to adapt and improve when given the right conditions. Nutrition plays a key role, as certain vitamins and compounds have been shown to support cognitive function and protect brain cells over time. Staying mentally active through reading, learning new skills, and social connection also helps keep the mind sharp. You deserve to feel focused, alert, and mentally strong at every stage of life.
