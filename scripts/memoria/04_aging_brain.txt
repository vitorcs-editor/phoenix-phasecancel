As we age, changes in memory and cognitive function are natural, but they do not have to define your daily experience. Research continues to show that lifestyle choices have a profound impact on how the brain ages. Regular physical activity increases blood flow to the brain and supports the growth of new neural connections. A diet rich in antioxidants, healthy fats, and key vitamins helps protect brain tissue from the damage caused by oxidative stress over time. Quality sleep is when the brain consolidates memories and clears out harmful waste products. By taking a proactive approach to brain health, many people find they can maintain strong mental performance well beyond what they expected.
