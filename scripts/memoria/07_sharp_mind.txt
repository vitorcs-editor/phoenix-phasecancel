Keeping a sharp mind as you get older is one of the greatest gifts you can give yourself and those around you. Being able to recall memories clearly, follow conversations with ease, and stay mentally engaged in the activities you love contributes directly to your sense of independence and confidence. The science of brain health has advanced significantly in recent years, revealing just how much influence we have over our own cognitive trajectory. From the foods we eat to the quality of our sleep and the level of mental stimulation we seek out, every choice we make either supports or undermines our long term brain health. Choose wisely and your mind will reward you for decades to come.
